# Code of Conduct

Hermes Gizmo is maintained for a technical community that should be safe, focused, and welcoming.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good faith.
- Keep bug reports and reviews specific, actionable, and evidence-based.
- Respect maintainers' time and support boundaries.
- Do not pressure contributors to reveal private logs, credentials, personal details, or production data.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, slurs, or personal attacks.
- Posting secrets, exploit details, or private data in public issues.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or repeated demands after a maintainer has set a boundary.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove disruptive content; close issues or PRs; block users; or move security-sensitive discussion to private channels. If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the repository maintainer privately.
